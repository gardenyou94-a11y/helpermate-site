2. Description of Service

HelperMate is a household management tool designed to facilitate coordination between employers ("Employer") and their Foreign Domestic Workers ("Helper" or "FDW") in Singapore. The App provides the following features:

  • Task management, weekly routines, and schedule coordination
  • Salary record-keeping and payment history (record tool only, not a payment processor)
  • MOM compliance tracking: Work Permit, 6-Month Medical Examination, and Passport expiry reminders with rest day recording
  • Expense recording with optional OCR receipt scanning (on-device processing)
  • Multilingual interface supporting multiple languages
  • SOS emergency contact shortcut
  • Rights and guidelines reference for Helpers

4. Employer and Helper Roles

The App is designed for use by two distinct roles within a household:

  • Employer: The household administrator who manages tasks, salary records, expenses, and MOM compliance tracking.
  • Helper: The Foreign Domestic Worker linked to the household who can view tasks, schedules, and access rights information.

Role assignment is determined at account creation and linked through the household invite code system. TEMFARMING does not verify the identity or employment status of users.

5. Household System

5.1 Creating a Household

An Employer may create a household within the App and generate an invite code to link with their Helper. The Employer who creates the household is the household administrator.

5.2 Joining a Household

A Helper joins a household by entering the invite code provided by their Employer. By joining, the Helper agrees to share certain data within the household as described in our Privacy Policy. A Helper may only be linked to one active household at a time.

5.3 Household Data and Dissolution

All household data (tasks, records) is shared between the linked Employer and Helper. Upon household dissolution (through account deletion, manual removal of all members, or mutual departure):

  • Shared household data will be archived for 30 days before permanent deletion
  • All household members will be notified 7 days in advance of permanent deletion
  • Each user's personal account data (profile, settings) is not affected by household dissolution
