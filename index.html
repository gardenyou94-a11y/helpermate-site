Why HelperMate?

Everything you need,
all in one place

Stop juggling between notes, spreadsheets, and WhatsApp. HelperMate brings all your household management into one app.

📋

Schedule & Tasks

Create weekday, Saturday, and Sunday schedules. Tasks auto-generate daily so your helper always knows what's next.

💰

Wallet & Expenses

Track petty cash top-ups and expenses. Snap receipts with built-in OCR. Every dollar is accounted for.

🏛️

MOM Compliance

Never miss a Work Permit renewal, medical exam, or passport expiry. Automatic alerts at 90, 30, and 7 days.

🤝

Household Linking

Connect with your helper via a simple invite code. Both see the same schedule and tasks in real time.

🌏

10 Languages

English, Bahasa Indonesia, Tagalog, Myanmar, Chinese, Thai, Vietnamese, Malay, Khmer, and Lao.

🔒

Secure & Private

Your data is encrypted and access-controlled. Only household members can see your information. PDPA compliant.
